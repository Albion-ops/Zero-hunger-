Summary of Challenges

Hunger persists because of food waste, poor distribution, conflict, climate shocks, and poverty. Perishable food is often lost due to lack of logistics; small producers lack market access; vulnerable populations cannot reliably access food.

How this system helps

  • Connect supply and demand: individuals and organisations can publish available food resources.
  • Reduce waste: encourages redistribution of surplus food rather than disposal.
  • Local action: makes it easier to identify nearby distribution points and donors.

This MVP can be extended with verification, geolocation, routing, and partner APIs.
